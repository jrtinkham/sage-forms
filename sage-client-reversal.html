Client Reversal Authorization

We request that you reverse or delete the entries set forth below. Each reversal or deletion is being requested to correct an erroneous credit to an employee's bank account and the amount being reversed/deleted is due and owing to us. If this request is received by you in time to permit you stop the payment to the affected employee, we request that you do so. If this request is received after such time, we request that you revers such payment by debiting the employee's account or accounts. We understand that requests for reversals must be received by Sage 50 Direct Deposit no later than the close of business on the fourth banking day following the date of the direct deposit into the employee's account. We also agree to inform each affected employee of the requested reversal no later than the "Settlement Date" of the reversal entry and of the reason for reversal. The "Settlement Date" of the reversing entry is generally the pay date of the payroll or the next banking day after our receipt of your request, whichever is later.

We acknowledge that you may be unable to successfully reverse an erroneous credit. We hereby agree to indemnify and hold you harmless from any liability, claim or damages that may result from your attempts to delete or revers the entries as requested by us.

We acknowledge that we will be charged a $35.00 processing fee for this request and that this fee will be debited directly from our bank account.

Please make copies, complete, and sign additional forms if more reversals are required. Complete and sign separate forms if reversals are required on additional companies. Please use one box per individual employee deposit reversal. If employee has multiple direct deposits to reverse, use multiple boxes. If a reversal is successful, it may take up to five business days to reimburse the funds.
